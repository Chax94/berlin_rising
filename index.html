Berlin Rising

A Film Directed by Vincent Janssen & Written by Srdan Jankovic

As an extremely infamous topic for students and adults, we bring you a social drama addressing the imperative matter of rent in the city of Berlin. Not only is this issue essential in Germany, but it covers all of Europe. From our base here in Berlin, we have gathered personal and public knowledge on this problem and used our artistic expression in the form of film to give a voice to the subject. Berlin, we have heard your cries!

Logline

As Berlin’s rent crisis continues to increase, so does the number of fleeing tenants. When this problem reaches Zayn’s own doorstep, the young artist decides to fight.

Vincent Janssen

Vincent Janssen is a director, writer, producer and creative artist born on March 12, 1998 in a town in the south of the Netherlands, Maastricht. After getting his first camera at the age of 13 he started making short movies. Later on Vincent started college where he studied Communication, International Event, Music & Entertainment Studies. While studying this bachelor's degree his attention went to the world of cinema. During his minors in Contemporary Scandinavian Fiction and Film Production in Lillehammer (Norway) his love for film expanded enormously. After the introduction to the art of filmmaking he continued his studies at the MET Film School in Berlin where he is now.

×

Srdan Jankovic

My name is Srđan Janković, I started making movies since I was a kid, and now finally I am a student at MET Film School Berlin. I took a long path to filmmaking. I was born and raised in Serbia where I graduated from Elementary and High School. After that I graduated from Mount Mercy University in the United States with a Business Marketing degree. However, the love for the film was still there. I quit my marketing job in the USA and came back to Serbia to work on a TV Series as a co-writer and 1st assistant director. Working there my love for the movies got even bigger, so I decided to study film here in Berlin. I believe that one day I will become a successful screenwriter and director.

Miriam Gurgutä

Miriam is a Romanian film student with a passion for documentaries and art house cinema. Developing a love for film from a young age, she has come to Berlin to study and pursue her career. Her first love in film was Pulp Fiction but over time she grew to love social dramas and independent films. After finishing her bachelors she hopes to become a producer and director in both fiction and documentary.

×

Mario R. Leon

Mario R. Leon is a Spanish upcoming talent based in Berlin. He was introduced to the world of art house cinema at a young age, jumpstarting his love for film. This led him to pursue the craft of acting. After film camps and classes he landed a secondary role on a Spanish TVE series at the age of 12. On completing his IGCSE’s in Mallorca, he moved to Vancouver to major in Drama studies for his last years of high school. Now living in Berlin, he is finishing his degree in the performing arts. Excited for the future, he is ready to take risks and determined to master his skills. Once he finalized his studies, Mario wishes to move to Australia or the USA to continue his acting career.
